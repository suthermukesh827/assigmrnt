Freelancing - Take Your Skills To The Market


In the current difficult situation with self isolation more and more people are
leaving offices for remote work. This short course will help you take your skills to
the market. We will train you to work on various freelance marketplaces like
Fiverr, Upwork, Guru, and other channels. This course is perfect for those who are
already skilled in their respective niche and want to grow their career.




















What will you learn?

What is freelance and how it is different from normal work.




All the pro and cons of working from home.




Types of work on internet.




How to choose niche and how to take your first order.




Filter projectsFilter projects correctly and choose adequate customers.
